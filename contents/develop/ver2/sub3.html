LADUREE® ORIGINAL

HEATED PINEAPPLE PUNCH

INGREDIENTS:

150 ml (3 parts) Malibu
200 ml (4 parts) Pineapple Juice
200 ml (4 parts) Boiling Water
50 ml (1 part) Fresh Lime Juice
25 ml (½ part) Sugar Syrup
Dash Angostura Bitters
Sliced lemons and oranges
1 x Malibu Tea/Spice Bag

HOW TO MIX IT:

Add the spice mix to the boiling water in a pan. Heat for 5 mins and then add the rest of the ingredients. Heat further until hot (but not boiling), serve in insulated punch glasses and dust with nutmeg.
